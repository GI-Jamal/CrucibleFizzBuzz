Bingo Bango

Bingo Bango is a Fizz Buzz like JavaScript application. Input a Bingo (Fizz) value and a Bango (Buzz) value to check a range of numbers and see if those inputs are factors of any of the numbers in that range. The range starts at 1 and ends at the number input as the stop value.
